What are Linear Gradients?

Linear gradients create smooth transitions between two or more colors along a straight line. They are commonly used for creating backgrounds, buttons, and other UI elements with visual appeal.

The syntax is: linear-gradient(direction, color1, color2, ...)

Direction can be specified as:

  • to top, to bottom, to left, to right
  • to top left, to top right, to bottom left, to bottom right
  • angles (e.g., 45deg, 90deg)
