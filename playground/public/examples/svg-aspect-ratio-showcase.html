SVG preserveAspectRatio Showcase

Each frame renders the same star path inside different viewports while changing preserveAspectRatio. The PDF backend should match the browser layout by honoring the alignment and meet/slice rules.

Property Preview Notes
preserveAspectRatio="none"
Shape stretches to fill the viewport, distorting the star horizontally.
preserveAspectRatio="xMidYMid meet"
Uniform scaling with letterboxing; extra space is evenly distributed.
preserveAspectRatio="xMaxYMid slice"
Shape remains proportional while cropping to fill height, anchored to the right edge.
