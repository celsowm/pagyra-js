Advanced Background Tiling

The space and round values for background-repeat provide sophisticated control over how background images tile within their containers.

  • space: Tiles are repeated with even spacing between them. The first and last tiles touch the container edges.
  • round: Tiles are scaled (stretched or shrunk) to fit perfectly without gaps or clipping.
